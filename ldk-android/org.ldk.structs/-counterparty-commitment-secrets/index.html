Implements the per-commitment secret storage scheme from [BOLT 3](https://github.com/lightningnetwork/lightning-rfc/blob/dcbf8583976df087c79c3ce0b535311212e6812d/03-transactions.md#efficient-per-commitment-secret-storage). Allows us to keep track of all of the revocation secrets of our counterparty in just 50*32 bytes or so.
